Suitywuty takes the privacy and data of users seriously. This privacy policy sets out how we may collect, use and handle the information you provide while you use our website and services. In using our website, you agree to all of our practices as set out in this policy.

1. Definition of personal data

Personal data refers to any information that can be used to identify an individual, including but not limited to your name, contact details, delivery address, and other information. At Suitywuty, we value the confidentiality and security of your personal data and are committed to handling it with proper upkeep.

2. Collection of personal data

We collect personal data when you use our website and services to process your rental orders. This may include:

  • · Your name, email address, phone number, and delivery address.
  • · Preferences for dress rentals, including the types of dresses you are interested in.

We collect this data when you place an order or contact us for any inquiries.

3. How we use your personal data

We collect personal information for the following purposes:

  • · Processing and fulfilling your rental orders
  • · Communication regarding the status of your order, delivery details, and issues related to your rental
  • · Improvement of our services and personalization of your shopping experience
  • · We do not use your data for unsolicited marketing, nor do we share your personal information with third parties for promotional purposes.

4. Children's use of our website

Our website is not intended for use by children under 13. Suitywuty does not knowingly collect any personal data from children. In the event we discover that we have collected information from a child under the age of 13, we will take appropriate action to delete such information in a timely manner. We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's activity online.

5. Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (PDPO) Compliance

Suitywuty is also compliant with the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance of Hong Kong, which governs collection, use, and storage of personal data. According to the PDPO, you have the right to:

  • · Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • · Request corrections to any inaccurate data.
  • · Withdraw consent for the use of your personal data, subject to certain legal limitations.
  • · To exercise these rights, or if you have any concerns about how your personal data is being used, please contact us.

6. Sharing of personal data

At Suitywuty, we respect your privacy. We do not share, sell, or lease your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes. Your information may only be shared with trusted service providers to fulfill your orders, such as delivery companies, but only to the extent necessary for that purpose.

7. Security of your data

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. No online service can be completely secure. Therefore, we strictly make sure that your information is protected. 

8. Updates to this privacy policy

Suitywuty has the right to change this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be published on our website, and the new policy will go into effect once it is published. We encourage you to check this policy regularly.

9. Contact us

If you have questions or concerns regarding your privacy or our practices, you can reach us at rent@suitywuty.com.
