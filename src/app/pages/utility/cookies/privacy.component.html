What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files left on your device whenever you visit a website. These cookies enable the website to recognize your device and store some information related to your visit, for example, your preferences or browsing behavior. Cookies enhance the performance, functionality, and user experience of a website.

How we use cookies

Suitywuty uses cookies for a variety of purposes, such as the following:

  • · Essential cookies: These allow sites to perform essential functions and features, such as: processing your orders and remembering your preferences during your browsing session.
  • · Performance and analytics cookies: These help us to understand the effectiveness of our website and how customers engage with our website, making other improvements to our content which provide a better experience for browsing and personalization.
  • · Functional cookies: They allow us to remember your preferences, for instance, your language preference or the fact that you have logged in so that you don't have to input it every time you visit.
  • · Advertising cookies (Optional): Though we don't do targeted advertising, third-party service providers might use cookies to track your activities across websites for advertising purposes. But these are not used by Suitywuty directly.

However, should you decide to disable the cookies, some features related to our website may malfunction, such as adding items to your rental cart and checking out.
