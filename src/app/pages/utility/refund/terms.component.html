At Suitywuty, we are committed to providing you with a reliable and seamless experience in dress rentals. Sometimes things just don't go as planned, so we have put together this Return and Refund Policy to ensure any issues are handled quickly and fairly.

1. Rental duration and condition

We request you to inspect the dress the moment you receive it. The dress should be returned in the same condition that it was delivered in. We are glad to take back returns only if the dress reaches you damaged or defective.

2. Returns

If you receive a defective or damaged dress, please get in touch with us within 24 hours of the delivery of your rental. After we confirm the fault, we will organize its return and provide a replacement or a full refund on the basis of availability and your preference. We cannot accept returns due to a change of mind, poor fit, or any other reason.

3. Refunds

Refunds are provided only on damaged or defective dresses. In case your rental is eligible for a refund, we will ensure it is returned to your bank account. Allow 4-5 business days for the refund to reach your account depending on the provider.

4. Late returns

Late fees will apply if the dress is returned after the agreed rental period. To avoid any additional charges, please ensure the dress is returned on time.

5. Exceptions

This policy does not apply to dresses that have been altered, damaged due to irresponsible handling, stained, or neglected. If the dress is returned in unsatisfactory condition, we reserve the right to refuse the return or refund.

6. Contact us

Please don't hesitate to drop an email to our support service if you have further inquiries about our return and refund policy or need help, please contact us at rent@suitywuty.com.
